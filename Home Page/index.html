Our Mission

Whispering Hills Community Finder strives to provide opportunities and information to people with special needs and their families. There has been a lack of recognition and connections within the special needs community, which leads people to start developing bad habits. The creator of Whispering Hills, Lisa Williams, noticed that the resources were scattered across town, hard to find, and not allowing these families to benefit fully. The goal of Whispering Hills Community Finder is to design a website that has all the connections, opportunities, and jobs in one place. The website accepts events and jobs and provides the relevant information to clients who have an account. This new community was built to foster connections, encourage support, and improve the quality of living.

Sponsors

Lisa Williams is the co-founder, who is a passionate mother who saw the changes that needed to happen in the town she just moved to. She knew there needed to be a system in which people with similar struggles could connect, and her drive to form this community was for her children. Her daughter had Down Syndrome, and after she moved to town, she noticed the lack of a community. She wanted to develop a Community Finder to create a hub where all connections and opportunities could be located easily.
